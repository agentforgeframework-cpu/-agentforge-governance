## Purpose

This document defines the core operational terminology used throughout AgentForge.

These terms should not be used interchangeably.

> Clear naming preserves operational clarity.

---

# AgentForge

AgentForge is an operational discipline for building, organizing, governing, and using AI-assisted operational systems.

---

# Standard

A Standard defines a rule, requirement, or shared operational expectation.

---

# Kit

> An AgentForge Kit is a governed operational collection assembled to help humans accomplish a defined kind of AI-assisted work.

---

# Tool

A Tool is a usable operational component within a kit.

---

# Workflow

A Workflow is an ordered or suggested sequence of operational actions.

---

# Project

A Project is a real-world operational effort where kits, tools, workflows, and standards are applied.

---

# Framework

The Framework is the organized structure of standards, patterns, templates, and guidance that supports building and using kits.

---

# Ecosystem

The Ecosystem is the larger living collection of:
- kits
- standards
- projects
- repositories
- examples
- operators
- practices
- communities

---

# Human-in-Command

Humans retain operational authority, judgment, and accountability.

---

# Governance

Governance supports operational clarity, reliability, maintainability, and trust.

---

# Production

> Trusted for intended operational use.

Production does not imply perfection.

---

# Composability

The ability for operational components to work together cleanly.

---

# Closing Principle

> Vocabulary drift kills ecosystems.
