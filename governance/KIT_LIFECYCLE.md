## Purpose

This document defines typical lifecycle stages used within AgentForge Kits.

---

# Lifecycle Stages

## Concept

Initial exploration and viability discovery.

---

## Alpha

Early operational implementation and testing.

---

## Beta

Operational refinement and broader usability testing.

---

## Production

Operationally trusted and approved for intended use.

---

# Promotion

Lifecycle promotion should occur deliberately and visibly.

The level of formality should match operational need.

---

# Human Authority

Humans determine:
- promotion criteria
- operational readiness
- governance expectations
- deployment approval

---

# Closing Principle

> Operational usefulness matters more than lifecycle theater.
