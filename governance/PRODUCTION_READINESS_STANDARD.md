## Purpose

This document defines the operational meaning of Production within AgentForge.

---

# Production Definition

> Production means: trusted for intended operational use.

Production does not imply perfection.

---

# Expected Characteristics

Production systems should normally demonstrate:
- understandable structure
- operational usefulness
- maintainability
- governance visibility
- sufficient documentation
- bounded operational behavior
- human approval

---

# Human Responsibility

Humans remain responsible for:
- operational acceptance
- deployment decisions
- governance decisions
- validation expectations
- accountability

If humans deploy immature systems, humans own those decisions.

---

# Closing Principle

> Production is an operational trust decision made by humans.
