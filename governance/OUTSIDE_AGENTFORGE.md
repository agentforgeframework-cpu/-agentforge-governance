## Purpose

This document defines how AgentForge relates to systems and environments outside AgentForge itself.

---

# Core Principle

> AgentForge is intended to support operational work, not isolate it.

---

# Interoperability

AgentForge Kits may:
- integrate with external tools
- operate across platforms
- use external repositories
- support external workflows
- interact with non-AgentForge systems

---

# Governance Boundaries

AgentForge governance applies only where humans choose to apply it.

Organizations and operators determine:
- where AgentForge is used
- what governance is applied
- what operational controls are required

---

# Portability

AgentForge favors portability where reasonably practical.

---

# Closing Principle

> AgentForge should help humans work effectively in the real world.
