# Human-in-Command Standard

Human-in-Command Standard (HIC-001)

Status: PRODUCTION  
Version: 1.0  
Applies To: Any system, workflow, organization, platform, automation, or operational environment involving delegated, assisted, or automated processes.

---

# Purpose

This standard defines how human authority, judgment, and operational responsibility are preserved within complex systems.

Human capability should be strengthened through disciplined use of tools, automation, and intelligent systems.

---

# Core Principle

> Systems may assist operations.  
> Humans retain operational authority, judgment, and accountability.

---

# Final Principle

> Human-in-Command is a foundational operational requirement for responsible systems.

---

# Notes

Implementation guidance, operational patterns, governance models, examples, and compliance interpretation may exist separately from this standard.

Those materials may expand upon this principle but do not replace or override it.
