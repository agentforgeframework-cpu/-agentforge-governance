## Purpose

This document defines what an AgentForge Kit is and establishes the foundational operational principles behind the concept.

---

# What Is an AgentForge Kit?

> An AgentForge Kit is a governed operational collection assembled to help humans accomplish a defined kind of AI-assisted work.

A kit is assembled around purpose.

It contains curated components organized to support real operational use.

Kits may include:
- tools
- workflows
- standards
- templates
- documentation
- examples
- supporting assets

Not every component must be used every time.

The purpose of a kit is to reduce friction between intent and action while preserving human judgment and operational control.

---

# Simple Human Definition

> A kit is a collection of things assembled to help people do a specific job.

---

# Core Principles

## Kits Equip Humans

> Kits equip humans; they do not replace them.

---

## Capability Drives Structure

> Capability drives structure, not the other way around.

---

## Governance Supports Operations

Governance should reduce operational friction, not create it.

---

## Kits Are Curated

Kits are curated operational collections.

They are not uncontrolled accumulations of files, prompts, or tools.

---

# Human Authority

Humans remain responsible for:
- authority
- approval
- governance
- accountability
- operational control

Organizations and operators determine how decisions are made within their own environments.

---

# Closing Principle

> A good kit minimizes friction between intent and action.
