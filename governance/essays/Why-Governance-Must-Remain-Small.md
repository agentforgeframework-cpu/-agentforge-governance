# Why Governance Must Remain Small

Status: DRAFT  
Scope: Ecosystem  
Author: Paul McDonald  
Date: 2026-05-10

---

# Purpose

This essay explains why lightweight governance is important for operational survivability.

---

# Governance Inflation

Governance systems naturally expand over time.

Every:
- problem
- exception
- failure
- disagreement

creates pressure for additional rules.

---

# The Danger

Large governance systems often become:
- difficult to understand
- difficult to maintain
- difficult to follow
- politically complex

Governance can eventually become operational friction.

---

# Small Governance Survives Better

Smaller governance systems are often:
- clearer
- more durable
- easier to teach
- easier to preserve

---

# Final Thought

Governance should reduce operational chaos, not create it.
