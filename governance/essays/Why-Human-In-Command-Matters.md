Status: DRAFT

---

# Purpose

This essay explains the reasoning behind the Human-in-Command philosophy within AgentForge.

---

# Human Authority Matters

Operational systems increasingly involve:
- automation
- orchestration
- AI assistance
- remote dependencies
- abstraction layers

Human-in-Command exists to preserve:
- visible responsibility
- meaningful oversight
- operational accountability
- survivability

---

# AI Assistance Is Not The Problem

AI may:
- assist
- analyze
- recommend
- explain
- troubleshoot

The problem emerges when:
- responsibility disappears
- authority becomes unclear
- dependency chains become invisible
- nobody understands the system

---

# Human In Command Is Operational

Human-in-Command is not anti-AI.

It is pro-accountability.

---

# Final Thought

A system nobody understands is difficult to trust, maintain, or survive.
