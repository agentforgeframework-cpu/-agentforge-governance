# Governance Vs Guidance

Status: DRAFT  
Scope: Ecosystem  
Author: Paul McDonald  
Date: 2026-05-10

---

# Purpose

This essay explores the distinction between governance and guidance within AgentForge.

---

# Governance

Governance defines:
- authority
- responsibility
- operational boundaries
- accountability

Governance should remain:
- small
- durable
- understandable

---

# Guidance

Guidance explains:
- reasoning
- tradeoffs
- operational lessons
- interpretation
- survivability concerns

Guidance may evolve more rapidly than governance.

---

# The Risk

Ecosystems become unhealthy when:
- guidance silently becomes policy
- interpretation becomes hidden authority
- every useful idea becomes governance

---

# Final Thought

Not every good idea should become a rule.
