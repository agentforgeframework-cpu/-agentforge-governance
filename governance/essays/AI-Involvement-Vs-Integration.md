# AI Involvement Vs Integration

Status: DRAFT  
Scope: Ecosystem  
Author: Paul McDonald  
Date: 2026-05-10

---

# Purpose

This essay explores the distinction between AI involvement around kits and AI integration directly into kits.

---

# The Distinction Matters

AI involvement around kits means:
- AI assists design
- AI assists validation
- AI assists troubleshooting
- AI assists onboarding

AI integration into kits means:
- AI becomes a runtime dependency
- AI becomes part of operational execution
- AI becomes embedded infrastructure

---

# Neither Approach Is Automatically Wrong

Some systems may legitimately require embedded AI.

Others may function better when AI remains external and optional.

The important question is whether the dependency is:
- visible
- intentional
- operationally justified

---

# Final Thought

The governance target is not AI itself.

The governance target is unconscious operational dependency accumulation.
