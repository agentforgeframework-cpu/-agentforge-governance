## Purpose

This document defines governance tiers used within AgentForge.

Governance tiers communicate operational maturity and intended trust level.

---

# Governance Tiers

## Concept

Exploratory or early-stage work.

---

## Alpha

Early operational implementation with active development and instability expected.

---

## Beta

Operationally usable with known limitations and ongoing refinement.

---

## Production

> Trusted for intended operational use.

Production requires human approval.

---

# Governance Authority

Humans determine:
- approval
- deployment
- operational trust
- governance expectations

Organizations determine how governance decisions are made within their own environments.

---

# Closing Principle

> Governance should increase clarity, not burden.
